Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2025

1. Introduction

FluxiPlay ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile games and visit our website.

By using our games or website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Currently: Our website does not collect any personal information. We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking tools.

In Our Future Games: We may collect the following types of information:

  • • Game Progress Data: Your game scores, levels completed, and achievements
  • • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • • Usage Data: How you interact with our games, time spent playing, and features used
  • • Crash and Performance Data: Technical information to help us fix bugs and improve game performance

Note: We will never collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it to us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • • Provide and maintain our games
  • • Save your game progress and preferences
  • • Improve our games and fix technical issues
  • • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • • Ensure the security and integrity of our games

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our games may use third-party services such as Google Play Games Services for achievements and leaderboards. These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your information. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before using these features.

We do not integrate advertising networks or analytics services that track users across apps or websites.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain game data only as long as necessary to provide our services and improve our games. Game progress data is typically stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the game.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are designed for general audiences and are not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • • Access and review any personal information we may have about you
  • • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • • Request deletion of your data
  • • Object to the processing of your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at games@fluxiplay.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
